### [Quantitative Alpha Engine](https://github.com/FardeenIdrus/Technical-Analyst-Agent)
- Implements 10-factor momentum and mean-reversion framework using RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, ATR, and moving average crossovers with weighted confluence scoring to generate high-conviction signals
- Detects market regimes through Hurst Exponent analysis (distinguishing trending vs. mean-reverting environments) and variance changepoint detection to adapt strategy behavior dynamically
- Validates edge through walk-forward optimization across 10 years of historical data, then stress-tests with 1,000-iteration Monte Carlo simulation using block bootstrap resampling to measure statistical robustness
- Optimizes capital deployment via fractional Kelly Criterion combined with GARCH(1,1) volatility forecasting to maximize risk-adjusted returns while preventing over-leverage
Generates institutional-grade investment memos with multi-scenario forward analysis, risk attribution, and drawdown projections powered by large language models
- **Tech:** Python, VectorBT, yfinance, pandas, scipy, statsmodels, Anthropic/OpenAI API, ReportLab
